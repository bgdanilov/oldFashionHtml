Welcome

We deliver top quality coffee to your home, workplace, friends, enemies, cats, dogs anyone you like. We deliver to all over Europe and soon hope to be servicing the states.

If you are looking for a special gift, then perhaps the subscriptionsection is worth a visit. This section allows you to arrange delivery of the finest quality fresh coffee each month. If you are interested in receiving a variety of coffee types then look at the coffee of the month subscription offer.

Coffee has a number of different tastes and tastes change all the time. The purpose of this website is to help you identify the coffee you like and then get your regular fix or once off order from us.

We offer a variety of coffees (we are adding all the time) that we feel are top quality. These coffees are sourced from importers and roasters directly. Most of them sell to trade only and their focus is on quality over quantity.

We feel that there is not enough choice in supermarkets for good coffee. What we offer is a number of different coffees, from different suppliers, from different continents, roasted to different strengths.

Our prices are competitive with the high street shops despite the fact that our suppliers are not producing in mass quantities and we have delivery costs to your door. Our suppliers trade on quality more than price, and we feel that you will stay with us once you find your ideal coffee.
