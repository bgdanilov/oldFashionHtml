Brookfield Village

Convenient student & self-catering accommodation in Cork City centre for UCC & CIT.

Student accommodation in Brookfield Village, Cork is in a class of its own: Purpose-built apartments and townhouses next door to University College Cork (UCC), or a 20 minute walk from Cork Institute of Technology (CIT), yet still very close to Cork city centre.

Move right in to a bright, clean, spacious room with facilities that are second-to-none, set in 11 acres of magnificent parkland which includes our Brookfield Leisure Centre.

No other student or self-catering holiday accommodation in Cork has such extensive facilities, including a 25m pool complete with waterslide, fully equipped gym, saunas, jacuzzi, rooftop hot tub, tennis courts, sunbeds and steam room.

Choose from 3, 4, 5, 6 and even 7 bedroom apartments and townhouses approved to Fáilte Ireland standard and featuring:

  • Fully fitted kitchen with cooker, hob, fridge freezer and microwave
  • TV
  • Bed linen and towels
  • Wireless broadband Internet access
  • Access to Brookfield Leisure Centre

Our buildings and grounds are warden patrolled for peace of mind and there is secure, off-street parking too.

Student accommodation

Student accommodation is available for the academic year from September to June with single rooms from just €60 per week.

Summer self-catering holiday accommodation

Self-catering holiday accommodation is available in July and August with family apartments from just €500 per week.
