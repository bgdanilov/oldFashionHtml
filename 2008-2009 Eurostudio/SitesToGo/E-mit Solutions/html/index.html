Our Business

E-MIT Solutions was established in 2003 to provide I.T. Consultancy services to small to medium sized businesses.

Since our launch we have specialised in providing businesses with professional I.T. Solutions. We have expanded our product range to include cutting edge technology such as Online Backup, Spam Filtering, Document Management and Managed Services.

As consultants we provide our clients with up to date information on emerging technologies and liaise with leading hardware and software companies to deliver a complete I.T. solution.

E-MIT Solutions have recently been included in the Law Society’s ‘Get Integrated’ publication* and are now officially recognised as I.T. Consultants to the legal profession.
