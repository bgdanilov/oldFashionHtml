Welcome to Ashleigh House B&B

Enjoy a warm welcome and a relaxing stay in our multiple award-winning family home situated on the N25, just 10 mins from Waterford City.

Guests can relax with a cup of tea/coffee in our TV lounge, catch the evening sun in our sunroom or take a stroll in our mature gardens. Enjoy a pint or a bite to eat in our local pub & restaurant, which is just a stone’s throw away or relax in our guest lounge. Browse through our library of books or catch up on emails with our complimentary wireless Internet.

Our guest accommodation has 6 ensuite rooms, all individually and tastefully decorated. A comfortable night’s sleep is assured and in the morning you can indulge in a mouth-watering breakfast chosen from our extensive breakfast menu.

Ideally located for Waterford City and surrounding area

  • Waterford City is only 4km away
  • Whitfield Clinic is just a short stroll.
  • Local beaches and a stunning coastal drive are 10mins from Ashleigh House B&B
  • Comeragh mountains, lakes and waterfalls all within 15mins
  • World acclaimed Mount Congreve Gardens 2km.
  • Excellent choice of restaurants and nightly entertainment all within 10 mins drive.
  • Waterford, Tramore, Faithlegg House, Waterford Castle and Waterford Municipal Golf Courses are all within 8km

At Ashleigh House you are assured of a warm and friendly welcome in a relaxed atmosphere. Phyllis & T.J. will be delighted to help you plan your visit and our family pets, Kerry & Molly look forward to meeting you.
