Abbey Court Hotel

The Abbey Court Hotel, Lodges & Trinity Leisure Spa, one of the Mid-West of Ireland’s leading 3* hotels. The hotel is located in Tipperary, in the town of Nenagh, a town bursting with life and steeped in history.

From the moment you step inside the Abbey Court Hotel you know that you are in a special place. Our ethos is to ensure our customers receive the best of Irish hospitality with professionalism and individual service.

What makes your stay at The Abbey Court Hotel so unique is the Hotels many splendid features. Whether you prefer a quiet moment absorbing the surroundings, or a leisurely stroll through the gardens, you'll find plenty of opportunities to have a superb experience.

Whether you're looking for a brief interlude in your busy day, or a day dedicated to relaxation and rejuvenation, or organising that very important Conference or Seminar, the Abbey Court Hotels wealth of experience and amenities offers something from everyone.
