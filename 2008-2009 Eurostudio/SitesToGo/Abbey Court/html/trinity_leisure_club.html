Trinity Leisure Club

The large state-of-the-art gymnasium is equipped with the absolute latest equipment, which is easy to use and designed to suit every individual need. Here at trinity leisure motivation and encouragement are what we are all about. Our experienced and friendly staff will attend to your every need.

With its magnificent surroundings and luxurious finish you can plunge into the sparkling waters, relax and enjoy our 20-metre swimming pool. Perfect for swimming endless lengths, taking part in aqua aerobics or simply a quick dip! The separate kiddie's pool is a safe and fun way to increase your child's confidence in the water. Alternatively allow the water jets ease away backaches in a jacuzzi.
