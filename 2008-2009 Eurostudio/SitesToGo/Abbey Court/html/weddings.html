Weddings

For every couple, your wedding is one of the most important days of your lives together and choosing your wedding venue is just as important. The experienced team at the Abbey Court Hotel continually strive to ensure that each Bride & Groom have a wonderful and memorable wedding reception.

The hotel’s stunning gardens and beautiful internal decor is the perfect backdrop for your wedding venue and album.

Our dedicated Wedding Co-ordinator will assist you with every aspect of your special day from the initial enquiry through to the day itself. Attention to detail is paramount and we want to ensure that you are free to relax and enjoy the moments while we take care of the details.

For more details on our wedding venue contact our Wedding Co-ordinator Joanne O Dwyer on 067 42151 or joanne@abbeycourt.ie.
