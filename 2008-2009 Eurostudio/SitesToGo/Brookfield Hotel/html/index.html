The Cork City centre hotel where great value is all part of the service

If you’relooking for a hotel in Cork city that offers great value for money, look no further.

Brookfield Lodge Hotel and Leisure Centre is set on 11 acres of magnificent parkland in the purpose-built Brookfield Village, very close to Cork city centre. This is a very private setting with ample secure car parking just off College Road beside the U.C.C campus. That means we’re a very short walk from the Bons Secours and Cork University hospitals and the business/commercial complexes on the Model Farm Road. Patrick’s Street and the city centre is a pleasant 20-minute stroll away.

This is a great hotel both for families and for business guests because it has everything you need, including:

  • 24 comfortably appointed rooms
  • Fully licensed bar
  • Restaurant serving evening meals
  • Complimentary Internet connection
  • Leisure Centre with 25m pool & gym
  • Secure complimentary off street parking
  • Self service laundry

A comfortable hotel, great location and low rates. How can we improve on that? How about FREE access to the Brookfield Leisure Centre next door? No other Cork hotel has such extensive facilities, including a 25m pool complete with waterslide, fully equipped gym, saunas, jacuzzi, rooftop hot tub, tennis courts, sunbeds and steam room.
