Welcome to Gowran Park

Located on the picturesque Lord Annaly Estate in the heart of County Kilkenny, Gowran Park is home to the only Grade 1 racecourse in the South East and since 2001, to one Ireland's Top 100 golf courses in Kilkenny.

With a complete upgrade of both racing and golfing facilities in 2001, Gowran Park is also the perfect venue for client entertainment, family celebrations, meetings, conferences, weddings and parties.

If you are a golfer, racing fan or both, we hope this website provides you with whatever information you require to enjoy Gowran Park at it's best.

The Club House Bar Restaurant is open to the public daily.

You can access the section of the website that interests you by clicking on one of the tabs above or via the links on the left of the page.
