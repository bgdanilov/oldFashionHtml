Welcome to FANAD HOUSE

Looking for a centrally located bed and breakfast in Kilkenny city, look no further. A warm welcome awaits you at Fanad House bed and breakfast, your home away from home.

All 12 rooms are en-suite with:

  • Tea/coffee facilities
  • TV
  • Phones
  • Hairdryer
  • Wi-Fi available throughout
  • Ample free private parking provided

Fanad House is home of Pat Wallace and family. We take pride in caring for our guests as though they were members of our own family.

Tastefully decorated, Fanad House Bed & Breakfast overlooks Kilkenny Castle Park and within a five minute walk of the city centre. Kilkenny is Irelands medieval capital and has many visitor attractions and fascinating historical buildings.
