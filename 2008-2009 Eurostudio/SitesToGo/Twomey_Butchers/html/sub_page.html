About

Now offering our top quality Irish meat online delivered direct to your door.

Products include our locally sourced Irish beef, lamb and pork cuts, seasonal ranges and bespoke cuts designed parties, BBQs and special occasions.

Twomeys are local Irish family butcher working with local Irish farmers to produce top quality beef, lamb, pork, poulry, hand made sausages & burgers.
