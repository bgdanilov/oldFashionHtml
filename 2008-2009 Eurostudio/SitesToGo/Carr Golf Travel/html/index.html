Founded in 1990, Carr Golf Travel has been inspiring unique golf vacations to Ireland, Scotland and select destinations worldwide. Perhaps your passion is for the dramatic golf vistas that are unique to Golfing in Ireland, like Ballybunion Golf Links or the Old Head of Kinsale, designed by our founder Joe Carr? Or your dream is to play golf at the Home of Golf, the Old Course at St. Andrews in Scotland or the British Open venues Turnberry, Troon or Carnoustie? Or how about a golf vacation to Pebble Beach Golf Links, the TPC Stadium Course at Sawgrass in the USA or the Green Monkey at Sandy Lane in Barbados?

The Carr family have been entertaining golfers and creating unique golf travel experiences for over 60 years.

Whether Golfing in Ireland, Golfing in Scotland or in any of our featured destinations, clients worldwide repeatedly turn to Carr Golf Travel to create unique golf travel experiences as well as to attend major golf events such as the British Open Golf Championship, the Ryder Cup, and our renowned World Invitational Father & Son Golf Tournament. Sister company Carr Corporate Events also arranges specialist client events for top companies worldwide. Challenge us to create your ultimate golf experience! With our in-depth knowledge of golf and working with the finest partners in the golf travel industry, we will create your most memorable golf vacation to date.
