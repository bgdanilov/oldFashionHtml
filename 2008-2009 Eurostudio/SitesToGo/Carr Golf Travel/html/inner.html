The Complete Golf Experience

Welcome to Carr Golf Travel! Legendary in Irish golfing circles, the Carr family has been welcoming golf travellers to Ireland and Scotland for over 60 years.

The business is also renowned for its annual golfing tournaments. From The World Invitational Father & Son Golf Tournament to The Sawgrass International Golf Championship, we guarantee participants a fantastic combination of highly enjoyable golf, beautiful locations and excellent entertainment.

«The Father & Son Tournament embodies the spirit and attitude of the Carr family which is good golf, great friendships and good craic (fun)»

Dermot Desmond (three time winner of the event)

While the travel business initially focussed on Ireland and Scotland, over the years we have broadened our offering to include new geographic markets with the discovery of hidden gems. Carr Golf Travel has become a global force in the industry, offering a range of exclusive destinations across Ireland, the UK, Europe, US, Asia and beyond. Regardless of location, our dedicated team will always strive to exceed expectations through superior service levels, tried and tested partners, attention to detail, industry expertise and continued focus on customer service excellence.

In addition to our renowned travel businesses, the Carr Group provides a diversified offering to include corporate and incentive travel through our Carr Corporate Events division in addition to golf course management and advisory services through Carr Golf Services.

Please browse through our website to discover how Carr Golf Travel can work for you: whether organising golf vacations; corporate programs; participating in one of our unique tournaments; organising packages for such events as Ryder Cup; or brushing up on your game with the Dave Pelz Golf School. Carr Golf Travel guarantees the ultimate golfing experience.

I’m Marty Carr and I’d like to introduce you to Carr Golf Travel, an international travel business specialising in tailor-made golf vacations to suit all budgets. Our family company has been assisting clients from all over the world in the delivery of unique golf experiences as well as handling momentous events such as the Ryder Cup and Solheim Cup.
